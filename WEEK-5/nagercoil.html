Nagercoil

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Nagercoil campus (ACET) is a 100+ acre, NAAC A++
accredited institution in Erachakulam, focusing on engineering (B.Tech/M.E.) and technology.
It offers modern labs, hostels, and sports facilities. With a strong focus on research,
it is part of a top-ranked, multi-disciplinary university system, offering holistic
education with an average placement salary around ₹9.08 LPA.

Placements & Recognition: The campus boasts strong industry connections, with
top recruiters including TCS, Cognizant, Wipro, and HCL. As part of the
Amrita system, it benefits from high rankings (NAAC A++).

Culture: The campus is known for a "culture of care" that blends
academic, technical training with spiritual, cultural,
and personal development (e.g., yoga, coding clubs)
