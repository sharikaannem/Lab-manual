Coimbatore

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Coimbatore campus, established in
1994 at Ettimadai, is a premier, NAAC A++ accredited, multi-
disciplinary research institution and the university's headquarters.
Set on a 400-acre site, it offers over 120 UG/PG/PhD programs to
12,000+ students, featuring top-tier engineering, business
(AACSB accredited), and science schools.

Location: Situated in a serene, 400-acre campus in Ettimadai, at the base of the Western Ghats.

Academic Focus: The campus is home to the Amrita School of Engineering (established 1994),
Amrita School of Business, and various Arts & Sciences departments.
