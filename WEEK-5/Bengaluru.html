Bengaluru

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Bengaluru campus, located in Kasavanahalli
off Sarjapura Road, is a 50-acre, top-ranked, research-intensive institution
featuring schools of Engineering and Business. It offers B.Tech, M.Tech,
and MBA programs, focusing on industry-oriented curriculum and strong placement
opportunities through its CIR department

Academic Offerings: Primarily known for its School of Engineering
(B.Tech, M.Tech) and School of Business (MBA), with a focus
on research, modern laboratories, and specialized centers.

Location & Atmosphere: Situated in a peaceful"ashram-like" environment
in South Bengaluru, providing a green, serene setting for
study, while being close to major IT hubs like Electronic City.
