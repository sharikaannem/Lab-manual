About technology

Technology plays an important role in our daily lives. It refers to the
use of scientific knowledge, tools, and machines to solve problems and
make work easier. From smartphones and computers to medical equipment and
transportation systems, technology helps people live more comfortably and efficiently.

In today’s world, technology connects people globally through the internet
and social media. It supports education through online learning platforms
and improves healthcare with advanced medical devices. Businesses also
depend on technology to manage data, communicate, and grow faster

Although technology has many advantages, it should be used wisely. Overuse
can lead to problems such as reduced physical activity and privacy issues.
Therefore, balancing technology use is important.Overall, technology continues
to evolve and shape the future of society, making life smarter and more connected.
