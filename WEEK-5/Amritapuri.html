Amritapuri

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Amritapuri campus, located in Vallikavu,
Kerala, is an 80-acre, top-ranked, multidisciplinary hub (8th in India, NIRF
2025) known for engineering, research, and its scenic location between backwaters
and the sea. It offers courses in AI, robotics, engineering, and biotechnology, with
a strong focus on, social impact, and industry-aligned experiential learning.

Infrastructure & Location: Situated in Kollam district, the 80-acre campus
is bordered by a lagoon and the Arabian Sea. It includes modern amenities,
academic blocks, and student hostels.

Focus: The institution draws inspiration from Mata Amritanandamayi Devi and
focuses on combining education with compassion-driven research, such as renewable
energy and, sustainability initiatives like converting, agricultural waste
into carbon.
