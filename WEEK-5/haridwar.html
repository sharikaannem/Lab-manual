Haridwar

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's Haridwar campus, established in 2025 as its 10th campus,
is located on the banks of the Ganges in Uttarakhand. It offers specialized B.Tech programs
in Computer Science, AI, and Electronics, aiming to blend traditional values with modern
technology. It is a NAAC A++ accredited, private institution under the guidance of Mata Amritanandamayi Devi.

Campus Life & Philosophy: The campus reflects the vision of "education
for life" and "compassion-driven research". It incorporates value-based education,
and students are taught the Integrated Amrita Meditation (IAM) technique.

Admissions & Eligibility: Admissions are based on merit, specifically through AEEE
(Amrita Entrance Examination - Engineering) or JEE Main. Candidates require a
minimum of 50% in each subject (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) and 60% aggregate in PCM.
