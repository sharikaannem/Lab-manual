Amaravati

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham’s Amaravati campus (est. 2021-2022) is a 100-acre, NAAC A++
accredited, research-intensive institution in Andhra Pradesh focusing on technology and engineering.
Located near the Krishna River in Guntur, it offers B.Tech, M.Sc., and MBA programs with
modern infrastructure, high-speed Wi-Fi, and top placements reaching 40 LPA.

Academics & Courses: The campus offers B.Tech programs (Computer Science,
AI, Cyber Security, Computer & Communication Engineering) with a total fee
around ₹18.00 L for 4 years. Other programs include M.Sc. Data Science/Physics and MBA

Infrastructure: Located on a green campus, featuring modern labs,
research facilities, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
