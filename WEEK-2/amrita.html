Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

IQAC - Amrita Vishwa VidyapeethamAmrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a premier,
multi-disciplinary, private deemed-to-be-university in India, founded in 1994 by
Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (AMMA). Accredited with an 'A++' grade by NAAC
and ranked 8th in India by NIRF 2025, it operates across 10 campuses with over 30,000
students. The university offers 250+ programs in Engineering, Medicine, Management, and Arts.
